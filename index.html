About GlowByte

Built for gamers,
open to everyone

GlowByte Cyber Cafe is located in a 15' × 25' shop lot in Ayer Keroh, Melaka. We offer a clean, air-conditioned space with fast internet, gaming PCs, and printing services — perfect for students, gamers, and anyone who needs reliable internet access.

Our network runs on TM UniFi Business 500 Mbps fibre across all 10 wired gaming stations and Wi-Fi for personal devices. Each gaming rig packs an Intel i7-13700F with RTX 3060 12GB — built for smooth, high-frame gameplay. Every PC uses Deep Freeze to keep things fresh between sessions.

Services

Tech Specs

Our Network

Built on enterprise-grade components for maximum reliability and speed. Every station is individually wired via CAT6 in a star topology.

Component Specification Role
Internet (WAN) TM UniFi Business 500 Mbps FTTH Fibre Main internet uplink
Wireless Router TP-Link Archer AX73 (Wi-Fi 6) DHCP, NAT, firewall, Wi-Fi
Switch TP-Link TL-SG1016D 16-port Gigabit Connects all wired PCs
Cabling CAT6 UTP, RJ-45 — star topology 1 Gbps per station
Topology Star topology IEEE 802.3ab Fault isolation per station
IP Scheme 192.168.1.0 / 24 (Class C private) Internal LAN addressing
Client PCs Intel i7-13700F, RTX 3060 12GB, 27" QHD 165Hz 10 gaming workstations
Security Deep Freeze + Avast Business Antivirus PC integrity & protection
Backup Power APC UPS 700VA × 2 Protection against power cuts
